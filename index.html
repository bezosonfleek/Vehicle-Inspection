Features

Driver compliance

Driver compliance entails the compulsory use of full protective gear for safety while driving, coupled with a strict adherence to sobriety guidelines. Additionally, it involves practicing a code of conduct to foster a respect.

Vehicle details

Vehicle details are essential for tracking and managing logistics efficiently. Crucial information such as the registration number and supplier name is recorded. These details enable effective monitoring and coordination and minimize potential risks.

Vehicle condition

Assessing vehicle condition involves inspecting critical components to ensure structural integrity and safety. It brings about proper functionality and secure attachment for efficient transportation.

Vehicle systems

Monitoring vehicle systems is crucial for ensuring safe operation. Proper maintenance of these systems enhances vehicle performance, safety, and reliability on the road.

Vehicle safety

Vehicle safety ensures the functionality of critical components such as indicator and brake lights or any leaks. Regular examination of these elements is vital in terms of security.

Vehicle accessories

Vehicle accessories like a first aid kit, fire extinguisher, and wheel chocks are essential safety equipment. These items ensure emergencies are under control.
